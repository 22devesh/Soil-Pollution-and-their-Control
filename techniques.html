Techniques of Control of Soil Pollution


The control of soil pollution can be done in various methods depending on a few factors. A few common methods include the following:



1. Soil remediation


This involves the process of removing or neutralizing contaminants in the soil. Techniques such as bioremediation, phytoremediation, and chemical treatment can be used for soil remediation.




2. Soil vapor extraction

This technique involves the use of a vacuum pump to extract volatile contaminants from the soil.




3. Phytostabilization

This involves the use of plants to stabilize and immobilize contaminants in the soil.




4. Monitoring and testing

This involves the regular monitoring and testing of soil quality to detect any changes or new contaminants.
